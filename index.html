About Jesse Dunn

I’m an Arizona native, born and raised in Phoenix, and now lucky to call Snowflake home.

I’ve been a licensed real estate agent for nearly two years, and in that time, I’ve developed a specialty in land, off-grid properties, and homes across Northern Arizona. I’m known for going above and beyond for my clients, whether they’re buying their first property or selling a piece of land that’s been in the family for generations.

I have a real passion for helping people through every step of the process. I still remember the excitement of buying my first home, and now I get to help others feel that same thrill.

Whether you’re buying, selling, or just want to talk real estate — I’d love to connect!
